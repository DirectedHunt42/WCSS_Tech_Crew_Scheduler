How to Use This Website

Welcome to the WCSS Tech Booking Site! Follow these steps to book an event:

  1. Go to Book a tech Event in the sidebar or click on the day of your event on the Calendar and click the "Book and event button"
  2. On the booking page, fill out the required fields:
    • Event Name: Enter the name of your event
    • Email Address: Provide a valid email address for communication.
    • Booking Date: Select the date of your event using the date picker.
    • Start Time: Enter the starting time for your event.
    • End Time: Enter the time your event will end at
    • Location: Enter the location of the event, e.g. 'Small Gym', 'Room 225', ect.
    • Types of People Needed: Enter the kind of people you will need, e.g. 'Sound', 'Lights', etc.
    • Voulenteer hours: This field will be autofiled, edit it if the event will be taking place during scholl hours
  3. Once all fields are completed, click the Submit Booking button to finalize your request.
  4. You will recive an email stating wether your request has been aprooved or denied
  5. If you need to return to the main page, click the Back to Main Page button.

If you encounter any issues, please contact the WCSS Tech Crew for assistance.
