How to Use This Website

Welcome to the WCSS Tech Booking Site! This page will help you book an event and understand what happens after you submit your request.

  1. Go to Book a Tech Event in the sidebar, or click on the day of your event on the Calendar and use the "Book an event" button.
  2. On the booking page, fill out the required fields:
    • Event Name: Enter the name of your event.
    • Email Address: Provide a valid email address for communication.
    • Booking Date: Select the date of your event using the date picker.
    • Start Time: Enter the starting time for your event.
    • End Time: Enter the time your event will end.
    • Location: Enter the location of the event (e.g. "Small Gym", "Room 225").
    • Types of People Needed: List the tech roles you need (e.g. "Sound", "Lights").
    • Volunteer Hours: This will be auto-filled based on your times, but you can edit it if your event is during school hours.
  3. Once all fields are completed, click the Submit Booking button to send your request.
  4. You will receive an email once your request has been approved or denied by an admin.
  5. If you need to return to the main page, click the Back to Main Page button.

If you encounter any issues, please contact the WCSS Tech Crew for assistance.

What Happens After You Submit?

  • Admin Review: Your booking request is sent to the Tech Crew admins for approval. They check for conflicts and ensure your event can be supported.
  • Member Sign-Ups: Once approved, Tech Crew members can see your event and "opt in" to help. Members choose which events and roles they want to support.
  • Communication: If more information is needed, admins may contact you using the email you provided.
  • Volunteer Assignment: The Tech Crew team will assign members to your event based on the roles you requested.
  • Event Status: You will be notified by email when your event is approved, and again if there are any changes or if your request is denied.

Other Notes

  • Editing or Cancelling: If you need to change or cancel your booking after submitting, please contact the Tech Crew directly.
  • Tech Crew Members: Only approved members can see the full event list and sign up for events. As a non-member, you only need to use the booking form and check your email for updates.
  • Need Help? If you're unsure about any step, don't hesitate to reach out to the Tech Crew for guidance.
